About us

UNIVERSAL CONSULTANCY is the first most exciting and professional theme building company in UAE. We specialize in a variety of art applications like designing and fabricating and entire environment with a focused theme. We can create the wildest thought to reality in 3D displays, no matter how tough it looks or how big it is.

UNIVERSAL CONSULTANCY serves a wide range of clients including theme parks, hotels, residences, restaurants & resorts, worship areas, event companies, architectural firms and corporations, museums, malls and retail stores, advertising, public relations and marketing agencies.

We work with organizations of every size and in all parts of the country and world.
